About Me

Hello! I am Hinotori. Singuler They/Xe. Japanese Filipino. Mixed. Person of Color. Agender. Genderless. Queer. Autistic. Disabled. Those are all words that can be used to describe me as a person, but my favorite word I use to introduce myself is Artist. I am an Artist in every way, shape, and for imaginable. "A Jack Of All Art Trades, Master Of Some" is my personal quote and what I think reflects me as a creative mind the most.

Experience

I've loved being creative ever since I was a wee lad. Smearing paint on paper, making swirl patterns with crayons and sticking stickers literally anywhere I could stick them. Even to this day, I'm just a kid who loves to daydream about wonderous possibilities, and then I make those daydreams reality! I love creating art that helps link us humans for being human. We are all unique and diverse in our own right, but only the best art can help break down those boundaries and have us look at each other, human to human.

Skills

I'm always open to trying something new, especially when it relates to an art form. I love working with clients to help them make their visions come to life, as well as working on teams so that I can meet and talk with other artists! I also love to plan and organize a LOT. Expect me to ask a lot of questions for clarification.
